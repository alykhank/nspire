Our Initiatives

Nspire strives to create and present unique initiatives that ignite the spark and fuel the fire.

NspireTV (NTV) brings you insights on today's market in business and technology. Latest trends and insightful topics will be discussed by industry professionals for global amplification of innovation.

The National Business and Technology Conference (NBTC) is Nspire's annual flagship event that is also Canada's largest business and technology conference. A two day conference, gathering over 400 delegates across North America. Key highlights include an entrepreneurship/consulting competition, keynote speakers, workshops, career fairs, and an exclusive intimate evening reception.

Discovery Series (DS) is a monthly networking event where successful professionals share their stories and experiences with young leaders. Our audience include the University of Toronto, Western University, University of Waterloo, and Wilfrid Laurier University.
