What is it?

Think you’ve found a great market opportunity? Want to find the perfect team to build it into a business? Discovery series Toronto and the University of Toronto Entrepreneurship Hatchery present the Accelerator Weekend! Entrepreneurial U of T students, in teams of four, will work to build the best startup, competing for the first place $500 cash prize.

Teams and Prizes

Got a team? Awesome. Still looking for some more people? No problem, we’ll hook you up. Teams of four will present to the Judge’s panel on the final day for big prizes:
1st place team: $500 cash
2nd place team: $300 cash
3rd place team: $200 cash
Consolation prizes for lower placing teams as well!

Event Details

Who: University of Toronto students
When: Friday January 17th 7pm to Saturday January 18th 7pm
Where: U of T Fields Institute, 222 College St., U of T St. George Campus
Registration: $10 per person, tickets sold online only.
